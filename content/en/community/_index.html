Learn and Connect

The HCX Protocol is an open and collaborative initiative. We encourage diverse stakeholders including insurers, policyholders, service providers, and regulators to join our growing community. Together, we can shape the future of insurance claims processing, foster innovation, and deliver greater value to policyholders.

Community Guidelines

We are an open community, hence it is very important that we all follow these community guidelines and code of conduct.

Respect and Kindness

Treat all community members with respect and kindness. Engage in constructive discussions and avoid personal attacks or offensive language. Avoid engaging in any form of harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior.

Collaboration and Openness

Foster a culture of collaboration and openness. Share ideas, knowledge, and resources to benefit the entire community.

Inclusivity and Diversity

Embrace diversity and create a safe environment where everyone feels valued and heard, regardless of their background or identity.

Responsibility in Sharing

Share accurate and reliable information and respect copyright and intellectual property rights. Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal or sensitive information without consent.

Constructive Feedback

Provide feedback that is constructive and respectful, focusing on ideas and solutions rather than criticizing individuals.

Help and Support

Support fellow community members, offer guidance, and collaborate on projects and initiatives. Lend a helping hand to create a supportive atmosphere.

Code of Conduct

Our community adheres to the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct. This code of conduct sets the standards for respectful and inclusive behavior within our community, both online and offline.

Please familiarize yourself with the code of conduct and ensure that your contributions align with its principles. If you witness or experience any behavior that violates the code of conduct, please report it to the community organizers promptly.

By participating in our community, you agree to abide by the community guidelines and the code of conduct, fostering an inclusive and collaborative environment for all.
